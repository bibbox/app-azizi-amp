About Us

The African Dairy Genetic Gain (ADGG) is an International Livestock Research Institute (ILRI)-led investment by the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation (BMGF) that is developing and testing a multi-country genetic gains platform that uses on-farm performance information and basic genomic data to identify and provide superior cross-bred bulls for artificial insemination (AI) delivery and planned natural mating to smallholder farmers in Africa.

Performance Recording

Establishing performance recording and sampling systems in Tanzania and Ethiopia.

Learn More

Feedback Systems

Piloting farmer-feedback systems that assist farmers to improve their productivity.

Learn More

Genetic Selection

Using the information and samples to develop systems to select cross-bred bulls and cows of superior genetic merit for AI and natural mating.
